Must Do Activities in Switzerland

Hiking

Switzerland is the greatest hiking and walking destination in the world. The land is crisscrossed by more than 40,000 miles (65,000 km) of marked hiking and walking paths, of which over 13,600 miles (22,000 km) are mountain trails. Hiking and walking is a way of life in Switzerland, where nearly half of the Swiss population hikes regularly. Hiking is one of the favorite pastimes in this country where over 80% of the Swiss population use the nation’s network of footpaths for hiking, running, walking, and other activities. On average, Swiss hikers enjoy 20 hikes per year, each averaging about three hours. The price of maintaining one kilometer of trail for a year is about $800—not cheap—but the Swiss see that as a great investment in quality of life and personal well-being. Switzerland's superb network of hiking trails is a source of natioal pride and civic enjoyment. Alpenwild, with over 20 years of experience guiding trips in Switzerland, offers a variety of hiking, trekking, and walking tours in the Alps. We know all the best spots in the Alps, ensuring a trip of a lifetime.
