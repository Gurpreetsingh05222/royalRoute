About Switzerland

Switzerland is the tourism and diplomatic capital of the world and the Swiss have separate and distinct stances in religion, politics and culture. This multicultural country has Germany as its northern neighbour, Austria and the Liechtenstein as its Eastern neighbour, Italy as its Southern neighbour and France as its western neighbour.

Travelling for Switzerland is going across mountains and lakes. Any season is beautiful to come to this small country in central Europe.Switzerland is one of the most mountainous countries in Europe. The Swiss Alps cover 70% of its land area and together with the lakes and rivers are the main tourists places.n its mountainous terrain are lakes and rivers ideal for water sports. Even some of them, have beautiful waterfalls that blend with the colors of the thick vegetation that is around.The main economic engine of the Alps is, without doubt, the practice of skiing in Switzerland. There are numerous ski slopes that are considered the best in the world for its facilities and its natural beauty.
